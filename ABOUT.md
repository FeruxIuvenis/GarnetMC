# GarnetMC
**GarnetMC** is a Minecraft 1.21.1 mod made with Fabric.

The main purpose of the mod is to add a new ore, structures, mobs and last-game boss.
Garnet is pink-reddish ore found in deepslate and rarely in stone. Can be found in loot chests.

Things **GarnetMC** adds:
* Garnet Ingot
* Raw Garnet Ore
* Garnet and Raw Garnet Blocks
* Garnet tools
* Garnet weapons and tools that are made of Garnet Ingot
* Special Garnet weapons and tools that were once made by Garnet Kingdom or the Garnet God(these special tools and weapons are once per world)
* Garnet armors
* Garnet mobs
* Garnet village, buildings and the King's castle(once per world)
